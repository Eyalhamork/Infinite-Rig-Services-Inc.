INFINITE RIG SERVICES, INC. (IRSI)
CORPORATE POLICY STATEMENT

1. Introduction
Infinite Rig Services, Inc. (IRSI) is a Liberian-owned company committed to providing high-quality, safe, and efficient offshore support services, supply operations, and manning solutions to the oil and gas industry. Our operations are guided by integrity, compliance, professionalism, and a dedication to local participation.

2. Purpose
This policy defines the principles and standards that govern IRSI's business operations, employee conduct, and relationships with clients, partners, and the wider community.

3. Vision
To be Liberia's leading and most trusted offshore support company, delivering world-class services that enhance the nation's participation in the global oil and gas industry.

4. Mission
To provide reliable, efficient, and safe offshore services through innovation, skilled workforce, and adherence to local content and environmental sustainability principles.

5. Core Values
• Integrity: Conduct all business with honesty and transparency.
• Safety: Prioritize the health and safety of all employees and stakeholders.
• Quality: Deliver services that meet or exceed client expectations.
• Local Empowerment: Promote the employment and training of Liberians.
• Sustainability: Operate responsibly to protect the environment.

6. Compliance & Ethics
All employees and contractors must comply with Liberian laws, international maritime regulations, and company standards. Corruption, discrimination, or any unethical conduct is strictly prohibited.

11. Implementation & Review
This policy is communicated to all employees and stakeholders. It will be reviewed annually to ensure alignment with national regulations, client requirements, and industry best practices.
