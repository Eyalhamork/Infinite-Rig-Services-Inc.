INFINITE RIG SERVICES, INC.
DEPARTMENTAL FUNCTIONS

1. Chief Executive Officer (CEO)
• Provides strategic leadership
• Oversees governance
• Ensures compliance
• Manages and coordinates all departments

2. Deputy CEO / General Manager
• Manages daily operations
• Implements policies
• Coordinates offshore manning, contracts, safety, and crew performance

3. Operations Department
• Manages offshore operations
• Coordinates technical support
• Ensures operational efficiency and safety standards

4. HR & Administration
• Recruits, trains, and manages staff
• Ensures compliance with local content regulations
• Sources and delivers equipment, tools, and materials for rig operations

5. Procurement & Supply
• Sources materials and equipment
• Manages vendor relationships
• Ensures supply chain efficiency

6. Logistics & Transportation
• Manages shorebase, transport, and cargo to and from offshore rigs
• Coordinates supply chain logistics
• Manages crew changing, cargo handling

7. Finance & Accounts
• Handles budgeting, reporting, auditing, and financial planning
• Manages company financial resources
• Ensures financial compliance

8. Technical & Maintenance
• Provides technical support
• Manages repairs and maintenance of tools and machinery
• Ensures equipment integrity

9. HSE (Health, Safety & Environment) Department
• Implements safety standards
• Conducts training
• Ensures offshore Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE) compliance
• Manages risk assessments

10. Support Units
• Legal & Compliance Advisor: Ensures contracts and operations comply with laws and local content regulations
• IT & Communications Officer: Maintains communication systems between shorebase and offshore sites
• Public Relations/Community Liaison Officer: Manages stakeholder relations and community engagement
