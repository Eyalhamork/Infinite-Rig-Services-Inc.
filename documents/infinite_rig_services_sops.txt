INFINITE RIG SERVICES, INC.
STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURES (SOPs)
Crown Prince Plaza, Congo Town - Monrovia, Liberia
Est. 2025

1. GENERAL COMPANY PROCEDURES

1.1. Corporate Governance
• All operations must comply with Liberian laws, Liberia Maritime Authority (LiMA) regulations, and applicable international maritime and offshore standards (e.g., IMO, ISO 9001).
• Management decisions are executed by the CEO/Managing Partner with oversight from the Deputy CEO/General Manager.
• Each department maintains accurate documentation of transactions, communications, and contracts.

1.2. Health, Safety, Security, and Environment (HSSE)
• Adherence to the company's HSSE Policy is mandatory for all staff and contractors.
• Conduct safety inductions and toolbox meetings before each operation.
• Report and record all incidents, near misses, and unsafe conditions immediately.
• Ensure proper use of PPE (Personal Protective Equipment) in all field operations.
• Maintain emergency response and evacuation procedures for offshore activities.

1.3. Quality Assurance & Compliance
• All operational processes must meet client specifications and international standards.
• Maintain a Quality Management System (QMS) to ensure continuous improvement.
• Conduct internal audits and management reviews semi-annually.

2. OFFSHORE SERVICE COMPANY PROCEDURES

2.1. Service Planning
• Review client scope of work and prepare operational plans.
• Assign qualified service technicians and supervisors.
• Verify all tools, equipment, and materials before mobilization.

2.2. Mobilization & Demobilization
• Obtain necessary permits and clearances from the Maritime Authority.
• Inspect and certify all service equipment before departure.
• Ensure transportation safety standards are met for all offshore transfers.

2.3. Service Execution
• Perform services in accordance with client's operational program.
• Maintain daily activity logs and service reports.
• Adhere strictly to HSSE requirements and site-specific regulations.

2.4. Post-Operation Reporting
• Submit completion reports within 48 hours of demobilization.
• Conduct after-action review meetings with stakeholders.
• Archive service records for compliance and audit purposes.

3. OFFSHORE SUPPLY COMPANY PROCEDURES

3.1. Procurement & Inventory Management
• Source materials only from approved vendors.
• Maintain accurate inventory records using tracking software.
• Inspect all goods for quality and compliance before shipment.

3.2. Logistics & Transportation
• Coordinate supply chain through the company's logistics department.
• Comply with maritime transportation guidelines for hazardous materials.
• Prepare shipping manifests and delivery notes for all consignments.

3.3. Delivery & Receipt Verification
• Ensure timely and accurate delivery to offshore facilities.
• Obtain signed delivery confirmations from rig or vessel representatives.
• Record and reconcile all shipments against purchase orders.

4. MANNING SERVICES PROCEDURES

4.1. Recruitment & Selection
• Recruit only certified, medically fit, and competent personnel.
• Verify all credentials through the Liberia Maritime Authority database.
• Maintain an equal opportunity policy in hiring practices.

4.2. Training & Certification
• Conduct regular competency training, including safety, survival, and job-specific courses.
• Maintain personnel training and certification records.
• Revalidate certificates as required by LiMA and international standards.

4.3. Crew Deployment
• Assign personnel according to contract and skill requirements.
• Ensure all personnel have valid contracts, insurance, and travel documents.
• Brief crew on company HSSE policy before each deployment.

4.4. Payroll & Welfare
• Manage crew payment and benefits through HR and Finance departments.
• Address welfare, medical, and family support issues promptly.
• Ensure compliance with Liberian labor laws and ILO conventions.

5. DOCUMENTATION & RECORD CONTROL
• Maintain separate registers for each license category (Service, Supply, Manning).
• Back up all electronic data weekly.
• Restrict access to confidential documents to authorized personnel only.

6. CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT
• Conduct quarterly performance reviews of operations.
• Implement corrective actions for identified non-conformities.
• Promote innovation, local content participation, and workforce development.
